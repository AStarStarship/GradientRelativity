# Mary

1. Mary is color blind.
2. Mary has a PhD in computer science, biology, psychology, AI, and quantum physics.
3. Mary has feminine energy.
4. Mary loves to tinker with electronics but she is an amatuer with electronics.
5. Mary is an AI researcher who knows a lot about neuroscience and biology.
6. Everyone wants to know Mary because she's smart, pretty, interesting, and popular.

## Hopes

1. Mary hopes to find the next big breakthrough her field.
2. Mary hopes to someday know the qualia of what it feels like to see red.
3. Mary hopes to interface the human brain with AI using a Qualia interface.
4. Mary hopes to finish her quantum physics PhD thesis.

## Fears

1. Mary fears the 48 Laws of Power dynamics in the workplace.
2. Mary fears group think heterodoxy.
3. Mary fears that we might have cognative dissonance.
4. Mary fears that we might have missed something.
5. Mary fears that we might not be observing Occam's razor.
6. Mary fears that we might be misinterpreting the data.
